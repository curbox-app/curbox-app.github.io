About Curbox

Started by nethical in April 2024, Curbox is an open source initiative addressing the growing challenge of internet and social media overconsumption. Since living a normal life today requires being online, we build software to help you stay grounded and focused in an increasingly distracting digital landscape.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build and distribute tools that curb screen time and digital addiction for people of all ages. We believe that as internet usage grows, staying connected to reality should not be a luxury. The founding principle of our project is simple: software designed to reduce digital distraction must remain open and accessible to everyone, empowering you to maintain a perfect balance between the virtual and the real world.

Why Use Curbox?

  • Extensive blocking capabilities with extreme granularity
  • Free and open source
  • Privacy friendly
  • Built by the community, for the community

How It Works

Curbox uses Android's accessibility permissions to analyze your app usage patterns and block them based on your settings. While other apps might compromise your data, Curbox guarantees your usage analytics remain completely on your device. Our open source codebase ensures total transparency and privacy.
